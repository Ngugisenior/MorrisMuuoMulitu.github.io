  • You, the Kenyan people, spoke clearly. We want dignity; we want to put enough food on our tables, and we want a lower cost of living. I listened.
  • I came up with four responses to your concerns. I call them the Big Four: food security, affordable housing, manufacturing, and affordable healthcare for all.
  • I am dedicating the energy, time and resources of my Administration to the Big Four. The Big Four will create jobs, which will enable our people to meet their basic needs.

Big Four Action Plan

  • 1. Enhancing Manufacturing

  • 2. Food Security and Nutrition

  • 3. Universal Health Coverage

  • 4. Affordable Housing
